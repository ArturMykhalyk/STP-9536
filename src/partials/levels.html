Levels and Progression

Each region of the Crystal Temple contains a unique visual theme, challenge curve, and ambient story elements.

As you progress:

  • Puzzles become more strategic

  • Traps and cursed blocks appear

  • Time-based challenges and multi-board levels unlock

  • Hidden mini-levels reward exploration

Unlockable areas include:

  • Sapphire Hall

  • Emerald Basin

  • Ruby Forge

  • Amethyst Sanctum

  • The Heart of the Temple (final zone)

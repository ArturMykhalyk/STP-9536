Boosters and Power-Ups

Boosters in Crystal Temple aren’t just tools—they’re magical relics designed for specific puzzle types.

  • Crystal Bomb

    Clears nearby tiles in a burst of energy

  • Lightning Scroll

    Zaps rows or columns of crystals

  • Time Sigil

     Adds extra moves to challenging stages

  • Relic Swap

    Lets you rearrange key pieces to beat stuck levels

  • Artifact Combo

    Activates multiple relics at once for massive effects

Boosters can be earned through gameplay or collected via daily rewards.

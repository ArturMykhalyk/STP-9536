About the Game

Crystal Temple is a richly designed puzzle adventure where players swap, match, and explore their way through enchanted ruins.

With over 2000 hand-crafted levels, each stage reveals new crystal mechanics, obstacles, and surprises. The game blends classic match-3 gameplay with a layered temple exploration narrative, challenging players to collect artifacts, unlock chambers, and master the secrets buried in stone.

Whether you’re a puzzle lover or treasure hunter, Crystal Temple offers hours of adventure, mystery, and gameplay satisfaction.

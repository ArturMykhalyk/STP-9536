About the Game

Crystal Temple is a richly designed puzzle adventure where players swap, match, and explore their way through enchanted ruins.

With over 2000 hand-crafted levels, each stage reveals new crystal mechanics, obstacles, and surprises. The game blends classic match-3 gameplay with a layered temple exploration narrative, challenging players to collect artifacts, unlock chambers, and master the secrets buried in stone.

Whether you’re a puzzle lover or treasure hunter, Crystal Temple offers hours of adventure, mystery, and gameplay satisfaction.

How to Play

  • Swap Crystals

    Swap Crystals: Align 3 or more of the same type to clear them from the board.

    Tip: Look for the best matches!

  • Create Combos

     Match 4 or 5 to unleash devastating boosters.

    Pro Tip: Use boosters wisely!

  • Solve Level Goals

    Collect specific crystals, destroy ancient blocks, or reach score targets within limited moves.

    Goal: Clear the blocks!

  • Unlock Zones

    Adventure awaits!

Levels and Progression

Each region of the Crystal Temple contains a unique visual theme, challenge curve, and ambient story elements.

As you progress:

  • Time-based challenges and multi-board levels unlock

Unlockable areas include:

  • The Heart of the Temple (final zone)

Boosters and Power-Ups

Boosters in Crystal Temple aren’t just tools—they’re magical relics designed for specific puzzle types.

  • Crystal Bomb

    Clears nearby tiles in a burst of energy

  • Lightning Scroll

    Zaps rows or columns of crystals

  • Time Sigil

     Adds extra moves to challenging stages

  • Relic Swap

    Lets you rearrange key pieces to beat stuck levels

  • Artifact Combo

    Activates multiple relics at once for massive effects

Boosters can be earned through gameplay or collected via daily rewards.
